VIP Industries

About The Project

Our lens became the storyteller for VIP Industries, housing the vibrant subsidiary brands—Skybags and Carlton.


Scroll to Explore

The Concept

For Skybags, we orchestrated two compelling campaigns — #FindYourTribe for Friendship Day and a showcase of their trendy backpack collection.

For Carlton, we captured the elegance and functionality embedded in each business bag, in line with the ethos of working professionals.

The Result

The campaigns struck a chord with the audience, fostering a sense of belonging and celebration. It wasn't just about bags; it was about connections.

We're proud to have created visual tales that connected with the souls they were meant for.
